Spanish Comprehensible Input

Today, I am giving a much more relaxed article than yesterday. This is the 2nd article here (yay), and I want to talk about something I have been doing for a bit. I started trying to learn Spanish using only comprehensible input. Well, asterik on only. I have to take Spanish class this year, and it really messes with the comprehensible input. Thanks to the way school is set up, I will probably forget all the vocab I learned from the class, and I could replace it the comprehensible input vocab. I am watching Dreaming Spanish's videos for the comprehensible input. I really recommend them, because their input is very comprehensible. I watched another comprehensible input series for 29 days, doing one video a day. However, these videos ended up being incomprehensible input, because I couldn't understand the story most of the time. They got more odd and harder to follow as the videos went on. I am very happy with Dreaming Spanish videos because they are always comprehensible. I would recommend giving their videos a shot if you want to learn Spanish.
