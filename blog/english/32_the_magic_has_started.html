The Magic Has Started

Every day, I have watched ~30 minutes of Dreaming Spanish. I have a total of 33 hours, and I can tell that my Spanish has improved so much since I have started. I can understand small meanings of words in some media, but I am far from comprehending anything in Spanish. Even though it is hard, I have to continue. This is the best way to learn Spanish, and I have sunk a lot more time into this than any other language learning endeavor I have ever take.
